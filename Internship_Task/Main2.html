📃 About Us

Bruciato Food Factory (popularly known as BFF) serves Italian, American and Mexican food with a focus on fresh and local ingredients. The concept is brought to provide delicious comfort food in a cozy intimate setting with a unique Ambience. What makes us unique? Bruciato Food Factory, the first waiter-less order taking restaurant in Navi Mumbai, allows you to place your order through a conveyor belt made with bike chains. Since Bruciato Food Factory’s humble beginning in 2016, its growth has been inspired with the passion to cook and serve healthy food.

"Driven by Passion, Innovation & Service as our motto, you will experience only the best Food, Music, Ambience and Drinks here! Now, Get a fix for all your late night cravings!"
